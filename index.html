You Need to Know About BEEHIVE, the ‘Best’ Restaurant in the Aisa

In the 10 years that the list has existed, only eight different restaurants have taken the top sport, and this marks Beehive’s first time a top the ranking. The restaurant deemed to be the “aisa’s best,” at least in the eyes of the attention-grabbing cabal of jet-setters who vote on the annual World’s 50 Best Restaurants list. Our chefs serves dishes like anchovy fillets over fried anchovy skeletons with lemon, crab cannelloni, and lemons. The lemons are famous. As you might expect, it’s all very Mediterranean.There is a tasting menu available for lunch and dinner that costs 500INR. There’s also a “cheaper” lunch menu that goes for 300INR, though you can’t get it during peak seasons (like summer).No time soon. A quick glance at the restaurant’s website indicates available tables for two and three are currently nonexistent, and the first availability for a solo diner is in late October. If you can round up a crew of fellow high-rollers, you might be able to find something in late July. Must visit to have a greater experience.
